Lo Mein

Description

Lo mein recipe from Souped Up Recipes

Ingredients

  • 300g of fresh egg noodles
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 3 tsbp of peanut oil
  • 300 grams 10.6 of marinade beef
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 1/8 tsp of baking soda
  • 2 tbsp of cooking wine
  • 2 tbsp of cornstarch
  • Black pepper
  • 1 medium-sized onion sliced thinly
  • 3 pieces of scallion cut into 2 inches long pieces

Steps

  1. Slice beef into 1/8 of inches thick pieces.
  2. Marinade it with salt, baking soda, soy sauce, Chinese cooking wine, cornstarch, and some freshly grounded black pepper. Mix well and let it sit for 20 minutes.
  3. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook fresh egg noodles for 2-3 minutes.
  4. For the wok, turn the heat up to max and heat the wok until it is smoking hot. Add a generous amount of oil. Give it a toss and so the oil will create a slight non-stick surface on the bottom. Wait for the oil to be smoking again. Add the marinade beef, then stir and cook.
  5. When the beef is halfway cooked, you should still be able to see some pink color here and there. Add a bunch of sliced onion. Stir for another 15 seconds.
  6. By now, the noodles should be ready. Take them out and shake off the excess water. Throw a bunch of scallions into the wok along with the cooked noodles. Turn off the heat.
  7. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and dark soy sauce. Mix until the seasoning is well combined and you are done.
