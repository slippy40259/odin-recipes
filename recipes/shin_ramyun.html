Shin Ramyun

Description

My Korean instant noodle recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Shin Ramyun
  • 550 ml of water
  • 1-2 eggs

Steps

  1. Pour water into pot and heat up until boiling point
  2. Add the veggies, soup base, and noodles into boiling water
  3. The noodles will cook in 4 minutes, so get a bowl and crack some eggs in. With chopsticks, whisk the eggs
  4. With a minute left of cooking, add the eggs into the soup. Stir the eggs until everything is done cooking.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
