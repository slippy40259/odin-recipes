Fried Rice

Description

A vietnamese egg fried rice recipe from omgfood

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. neutral cooking oil
  • 8-10 shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 chopped yellow or sweet onion, chopped
  • 3 Chinese sausages, diced
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ½ tbsp granulated sugar
  • ½ tbsp white pepper
  • 6 cups cooked white rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 1-2 tsp fish sauce

Steps

  1. Heat a wok over medium-high heat and in one tbsp oil
  2. Add shrimp, stirring one to three minutes or until cooked through
  3. Add onion and cook for one minute
  4. Add sausages. Cook and stir for three minutes
  5. Add garlic and cook for one minute
  6. Add veggies and cook for two minutes
  7. Add the rice and mix with the veggies. Continue mixing until rice is broken down with no lumps
  8. Add shrimp back in and season with salt, sugar, chicken powder, and white pepper. Mix together well
  9. Push rice to the side and add in the remaining tablespoons of oil. Add the eggs. Stir them until they're half-cooked, then quickly stir in the rest of the food. Continue to stir for a minute, the egg will continue to cook as it's coating the rice mixture
  10. Remove from heat and stir in the fish sauce. Taste and add extra season if necessary
  11. Enjoy!
